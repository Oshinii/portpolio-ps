Learning Outcome

Food Festival

Our group of ten members organized an exciting Food Festival under the Professional Skills module at Cafe by Chandrapala, bringing together creativity, teamwork, and careful planning. The food stall offered a diverse menu with both local and international flavors, including spaghetti, watalappam, cake, fruit kebabs, mixed kebabs, iced coffee, and mojito, which gave customers a wide choice to enjoy.

We divided responsibilities among members to ensure smooth preparation, some tasks were handled a day before the event, such as sourcing ingredients and planning the layout, while others, including food arrangements and final touches, were completed on the morning of the festival. The decorations, entirely designed and executed by our team, added a colorful and festive vibe that attracted many visitors. This event allowed us to develop essential professional skills such as teamwork, time management, communication, and event coordination, while also giving us hands-on experience in managing a real-world project successfully.
