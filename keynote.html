Keynote Speech

The keynote sessions emphasized the multifaceted impact of technology and AI on global challenges, sustainability, and business. Prof. Mahesha Kapurubandara highlighted AI’s practical applications in agriculture, healthcare, and education, showing youth-led innovations boosting efficiency and resource management.


Mr. Kalana Muthumuni illustrated how AI can align profit-driven capitalism with environmental and social responsibility, giving businesses competitive advantages.


Mr. Edward Braund presented unconventional computing through biological and chemical systems, showcasing sustainable, self-repairing computational models. Overall, the talks demonstrated that technological advancement, environmental responsibility, and innovative computing are interconnected, offering solutions that are socially, economically, and ecologically impactful.
